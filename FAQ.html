FAQ Section

What is CX SMS API, and What can I use it for?

JioCX SMS API is a powerful tool that allows you to integrate SMS functionality into your applications or systems. It enables you to send single or multiple SMS messages to specific recipients, groups, or even international customers. Use it to enhance customer communication, send OTPs, and track delivery reports in real-time.
